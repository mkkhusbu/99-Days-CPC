About Us

We Are Very Skilled In Photography

Hi, I'm Angelique and photography is my passion! My father passed on the “photographer virus” to me. From analogue to digital. I have taken a number of courses and workshops and, above all, practiced a lot! My children are now used to modeling. Perhaps that is where the penchant for taking photos of children comes from. The pure appearance and enthusiasm of children. It's always fun! “Photography is the art of frozen time… the ability to store emotions and feelings within a frame.”
